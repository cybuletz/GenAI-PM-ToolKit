Feature: Notification Service
Original delivery: End of Sprint 12 (June 6)

Delay: 1 sprint (~2 weeks)

Root cause:
The Firebase staging environment was misconfigured — the project ID was pointing to production.
This blocked the developer (Mihai) for approximately 1.5 days while waiting for DevOps to resolve it (ticket INFRA-402).
The issue was resolved on Day 4 of Sprint 12, but by that point the sprint goal was no longer achievable.

Impact:
- Email and push notification delivery delayed by one sprint
- No impact on authentication module (delivered as planned)
- Downstream dependency: notification preferences UI now pushed to Q3

Mitigation taken:
- INFRA-402 resolved and staging environment validated
- DevOps team added env config to their sprint checklist going forward
- PM reviewed sprint commitment for Sprint 13 to ensure realistic target
